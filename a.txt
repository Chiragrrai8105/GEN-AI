Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science focused on creating systems that simulate human intelligence to perform tasks like reasoning, learning, and perception. Leveraging algorithms and data, it is a transformative technology that includes subfields such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning (using neural networks for complex patterns), and Natural Language Processing. 
AI is widely used in daily life for virtual assistants, personalized social media feeds, and navigation apps. It holds significant potential for enhancing healthcare diagnostics and productivity, while introducing challenges regarding algorithmic bias and privacy. Emerging trends show a shift toward "Agentic AI," where systems operate with higher autonomy to achieve goals.
